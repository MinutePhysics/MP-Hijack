The Problem

The internet is filled with ignorance and misinformation. While clicking through related video links on YouTube, there's no telling when you'll find yourself unwittingly watching a painfully mind-numbing video. When faced with this peril, under pressure, it can sometimes be difficult to know what to click next to free yourself from an intellectual black hole...

To see it in action, just click the big red button to make the bad video go away.

How does it work?

The Solution

For the good of human-kind, the MinutePhysics Labs have produced the MinutePhysics Panic Button.

Panic!

Once installed into your bookmarks bar, you're just one click away from fast acting, long lasting, intellectual relief. It will swiftly summon the MinutePhysics Supersheep to replace the unfortunate video with something of high educational value.

Usage Instructions

  1. Drag the Panic! link into your bookmark bar
  2. Visit a youtube video page
  3. Click the "Panic!" bookmark!
